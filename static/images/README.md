# Images Directory

Place your logo and other images here.

## Logo

Your Robot Forest logo should be placed at:
- `logo.png` - The logo you showed me (the minimalist gray "forest" pattern)

The logo is referenced in `hugo.toml` and will appear in the site header.

## Blog Post Images

For blog post featured images, place them here and reference them in your post front matter:

```yaml
featured_image: "/images/my-post-image.jpg"
```
